Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Digital Ventures ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the KaChat mobile application ("App"). By using KaChat, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google OAuth or phone number (OTP), we collect your email address or phone number and a display name.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you use the App, including which characters you chat with, number of messages sent, and in-app events (e.g., ad watched, character created).
  • Conversation Content: Messages you send to AI characters are processed to generate responses. Conversations are stored in our database to allow you to resume them.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (OS version, device type) collected through Firebase Analytics for crash reporting and performance monitoring.
  • Ad Interaction Data: If you watch rewarded video ads, Google AdMob may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the KaChat service
  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • To generate AI character responses tailored to your conversation
  • To track your daily message quota and bonus messages
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To analyze app usage and improve user experience via Firebase Analytics
  • To respond to reports of inappropriate content

3. Third-Party Services

KaChat uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication provider. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI engine that processes conversation messages. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Advertising platform for rewarded and interstitial ads. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics — App analytics and crash reporting. Privacy Policy
  • Twilio — SMS OTP delivery for phone authentication. Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure with the following protections:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) ensures users can only access their own data
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using device SecureStore
  • API keys and secrets are never stored in the app — only in server-side Edge Functions
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is stored beyond your email/phone and display name

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers listed in Section 3 (Supabase, Anthropic, Google, Firebase, Twilio) solely to operate the App
  • If required by law, court order, or government authority
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Digital Ventures or our users

6. User-Generated Content

KaChat allows users to create AI characters and share them on the Discovery Feed. By creating and publishing a character, you grant Digital Ventures a non-exclusive license to display that character within the App. You are responsible for ensuring your content does not violate our Terms of Service.

We reserve the right to remove any user-created content that is reported as inappropriate, offensive, or in violation of our policies.

7. Children's Privacy

KaChat is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at developers@digitalventuresfz.com and we will delete that information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information (you can change your display name in-app)
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Stop using the App at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at developers@digitalventuresfz.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Conversation data may be retained in anonymized form for AI quality improvement.

10. Advertising

KaChat uses Google AdMob to serve rewarded video ads and interstitial ads. AdMob may use device identifiers and other data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings under Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify users through the App or by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
